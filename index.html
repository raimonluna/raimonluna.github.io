Raimon Luna

Professor Lector · Universitat de Barcelona

I work on the dynamics of black holes and compact objects, holography in higher dimensions, and how machine learning can help us solve the equations of strong gravity. I currently teach and research at the Departament de Física Quàntica i Astrofísica, Universitat de Barcelona, after postdoctoral positions in Barcelona, Brussels, Lisbon, Aveiro, and Valencia.
