Theoretical & Numerical Relativity

Raimon Luna

Assistant Professor (Professor Lector) · Universitat de Barcelona

I work on the nonlinear dynamics of black holes and compact objects, AdS/CFT holography, and I study new numerical techniques that can help us solve the equations of strong gravity. I am currently an Assistant Professor in Theoretical Physics at the Department of Quantum Physics and Astrphysics of the University of Barcelona, after postdoctoral positions in Brussels, Lisbon, Valencia and Aveiro.
